Never Do Laundry Again

Save 3+ hours per week while Rinse cares for your clothes. Free pickup
and delivery plus next-day turn around. Plans start at just N7000/mo.

If it fits in the bag, we take care of it.

Pick your perfect plan by selecting the number of laundry bags you’ll need each month and enjoy service 7 days a week.

Returned next-day, ready to wear.

Your clothes get their own machine, are separated by color, cleaned according to your preferences, and delivered neatly folded – we even pair your socks.

Incredible convenience. Remarkable value.

Priced by the bag so you save.

Your bag fits two large laundry baskets’ worth of clothes. Flat pricing by the bag allows you to save up to 56% over Pay-As-You-Go.

Never service fees. Always next-day delivery.

All plans include free Next-Day Rush Delivery and zero service fees, which means you skip the standard $7.95 Service Fee on all Rinse services.

Unlimited Rollover!

Your unused bags roll over each month to be used at any time. Even unused bag space is banked and accumulates for free extra Rinse Repeat bags!
